    Medical Supplies¶

    Trauma and field-medical equipment. Mechanical rules for trauma items live with the Combat Medic designation; most items on this page are narrative/roleplay props - a medic uses them to examine, diagnose, treat, and document as much as to restore HP. See Gear Tags for tag definitions.

    Trauma Bag 3.5 kg stops-bleed, revives Combat-Medic issue; IFAK + Trauma Kit + Medical Grade Supply slot.
    Trauma Kit 1.5 kg stops-bleed, revives 5 uses; surgical-grade wound care.

    Diagnostics¶

    Tools for examining a patient. These rarely change HP; they feed the GM information and create roleplay scenes.

    Item Weight Tags Notes
    Stethoscope 0.15 kg - Lung and heart sounds; chest-wound assessment.
    Pulse oximeter (fingertip) 0.05 kg - SpO2 and heart rate; detects hypoxia from chest trauma.
    Blood-pressure cuff (manual) 0.3 kg - Sphygmomanometer; detects shock before it's obvious.
    Digital thermometer 0.05 kg - Core temp; infection, hypothermia, heat injury.
    Glucometer + test strips 0.1 kg - Blood sugar; diabetics, confused casualties.
    Otoscope + ophthalmoscope set 0.2 kg - Ear/eye exams; TBI pupil response.
    Butterfly iQ portable ultrasound 0.3 kg - Phone-tethered ultrasound; FAST exam for internal bleeding.
    Handheld EKG (AliveCor) 0.1 kg - Single-lead EKG; cardiac arrhythmia detection.
    Penlight 0.05 kg - Pupil response, throat exam.
