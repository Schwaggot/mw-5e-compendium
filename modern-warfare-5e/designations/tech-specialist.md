# Tech Specialist

**Proficiencies gained:**

- Level 1 training in Weapon Proficiency Training: Sub Machine Guns, Shotguns.
- Save Training Level 1 in Intelligence Saves.
- One level of training in Technology and Investigation.
- B1ARD Kit (Tools) - B-1 Advanced Recon Drone toolkit; the diagnostic kit, spare parts, micro-tools, and firmware
  loader used to field-service the B1-RD. Proficiency covers repair, upgrade installation, rotor and optics replacement,
  software reflashing, and battery management.

## B-1 Recon Drone Operator (3rd)

Gain a B-1 Advanced Recon Drone (B1-RD) if you don't have one, and:

- Mark one target within 120 ft of the B1-RD; ranged attacks against it have advantage.
- +5 to Repair rolls for a B1-RD.

During a long rest, modify your drone with a Technology (Int) DC 15 check. Pass = one upgrade. Fail = no upgrade, retry
next rest. Critical fail = drone unusable until next long rest.

- **Night Vision:** Darkvision 120 ft, low-light 240 ft.
- **Thermal:** Advantage spotting living creatures; disadvantage on all other sight perception rolls.
- **Focused Microphone:** Hear through drone via headphones or speaker.
- **Pistol Mount:** Mount a pistol on the drone. If you moved no more than 60 ft, make 1 ranged attack using operator
  DEX + Proficiency.
- **Stealth Kit:** Advantage on Stealth rolls while operating.
- **Armor Kit:** AC now 18.
- **Structural Support:** +HP equal to your level.

See also the [B-1 Recon Drone](../gear/drones.md#b-1-recon-drone) base stats.

## Tech Package (7th)

You gain:

- **3× Motion Sensors:** 2-inch, 4 mm thick discs that ping every 6 seconds when detecting movement within 20 ft. Bonus
  action to activate. Last 24 hours.
- **2× Seeker Grenades:** Sticky grenade requiring no attack so long as it can roll; moves 40 ft and self-detonates
  after 10 rounds.
- **5× Audio Bugs:** 1 cm microphones letting you hear within 20 ft. Bonus action to place. Last 8 hours.

## Specialization (10th)

Choose one of the following specializations.

### Drone Operator

Your B1-RD becomes an Enhanced B1-RD (E B1-RD):

- Mark targets equal to your INT bonus with your E B1-RD.
- The E B1-RD may have 2 Upgrades active at once.

### Signals Specialist

As a bonus action, jam enemy radio and digital comms within 60 ft for a number of rounds equal to your Proficiency
bonus. Uses per long rest equal to your Intelligence modifier. You may intercept radio traffic within 1 mile with a
Technology check. You have advantage on Technology checks to hack electronic locks, and may disable a hostile drone
within 60 ft by winning an opposed Intelligence check against its operator.

### Hacker

With access to a target device or its network, breach a computer, phone, or security system with a Technology check (DC
set by GM). You may plant persistent access that lets you reconnect later without re-hacking. As an action, disable all
networked cameras and electronic sensors within 120 ft for 1 minute.

## MacGyver (15th)

With a Drone Repair Kit, create or maintain basic Tech devices in the field - audio bugs, basic comms, fixing a radio,
etc. (GM approval; Technology check, DC by GM). Your B1-RD gains 1 permanent upgrade.

## Tech Master (18th)

- No movement test needed with your B1-RD.
- Double Proficiency bonus on all DEX saves and checks with the B1-RD.
- Advantage on all Tech checks.
