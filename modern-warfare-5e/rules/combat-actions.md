# Combat Actions

Homebrew rules, mods, and actions in combat.

**Firing into Melee:** Disadvantage on the attack. Natural 1 = no misfire; you hit a random target in the melee instead (GM randomizes).

## Automatic Firing Modes

When attacking with a weapon that has the Automatic property, you may use Spray, Riddle, or Controlled Burst.

**Spray:** As an action, choose adjacent targets up to Dex modifier. Apply -5 per target beyond the first. Consumes 1d4+2 rounds per target (roll per attack). On a hit, score one extra hit per 5 whole points your attack exceeded AC (max = rounds fired in the volley).

**Riddle:** As an action, choose volleys up to Dex modifier on a single target. Apply -5 per volley beyond first. Each volley consumes 1d4+2 rounds. Extra-hit rule as above.

**Controlled Burst:** One burst per attack available. -5 per range multiple. Consumes 1d4+2 rounds. Extra-hit rule applies.

## Burst Fire

For weapons with the burst property. One burst per attack. -5 per range multiple. Consumes 3 rounds. Extra-hit rule applies.

## Aim

Action: aim at one target. +2 to ranged attack rolls against that target. Only one target at a time; breaks when line of sight breaks.

## Ranged Opportunity Attack

If a creature you're aimed at moves more than 5 ft during its turn, you may use your reaction to make one ranged attack against it (within range and line of sight).

## Suppressive Fire

As an action: 3 rounds, 1 volley of 1d4+2, or 1 burst per target. Target creatures within 10 ft of each other, up to Dex modifier. Suppressed targets have disadvantage on ranged attacks and Wis (Perception) next turn. Cannot use with Manual Action weapons.
