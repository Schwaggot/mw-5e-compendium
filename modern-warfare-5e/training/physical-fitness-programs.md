# Physical Fitness Programs

Physical fitness programs cover gym time, PT, and obstacle-course work. They raise the three physical abilities, improve
physical saves and skills, and add specialist conditioning for running, climbing, and soaking damage.

## Weight Training 1-4

Hypertrophy and strength work. Each level increases your Strength score by 1 (respecting your racial or class maximum).

## Agility Training 1-4

Coordination, sprint drills, and reaction work. Each level increases your Dexterity score by 1 (respecting your racial
or class maximum).

## Fortitude Training 1-4

Conditioning and durability. Each level increases your Constitution score by 1 (respecting your racial or class
maximum).

## Physical Saves Training 1-4

*Each level may be taken once per save (STR, DEX, or CON).*

Focused work on resisting a specific kind of physical threat. Pick a save when you first take Level 1; further levels
apply to that same save. Take the track again from Level 1 for a different save.

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in the chosen save.
- **Level 2:** +1 to that save.
- **Level 3:** An additional +1 to that save (total +2 from this track).
- **Level 4:** Advantage on that save.

## Physical Skills Training 1-5

*Each level may be taken once per skill.*

Drills for a specific physical skill. Pick a skill when you first take Level 1; further levels apply to that same skill.
Take the track again from Level 1 for a different skill.

Eligible skills: Athletics, Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, or Stealth.

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in the chosen skill.
- **Level 2:** +1 to checks with that skill.
- **Level 3:** Once per long rest, reroll a critical failure (natural 1) on a check with that skill.
- **Level 4:** An additional +1 to checks (total +2).
- **Level 5:** The reroll is instead available once per short rest.

## Cardio Training 1-4

Endurance running and sustained-pace work. Increases overall mobility.

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Athletics. If already proficient, gain Expertise instead (double proficiency bonus).
- **Level 2:** +5 ft to your base movement.
- **Level 3:** Take the Dash action as a bonus action.
- **Level 4:** An additional +5 ft to your base movement (total +10 ft from this track).

## Obstacle Course Training 1-4

Navigating rough terrain: walls, ditches, doorways, broken ground. Makes you cheaper to move through three-dimensional
space.

- **Level 1:** Climbing does not cost extra movement.
- **Level 2:** Standing up from prone costs only 10 ft of movement (down from half your speed).
- **Level 3:** You need only a 5 ft running start (instead of 10 ft) to perform a running long or high jump.
- **Level 4:** Add +1d4 to all Acrobatics and Athletics checks.

## Resistance Training 1-6

Deliberate exposure to specific hazards - cold tubs, heat, electric work, shock drills - plus additional conditioning.
Odd levels grant typed resistance; even levels add durability.

- **Levels 1, 3, 5:** Gain resistance to one damage type of your choice (picked when that level is taken).
  See [Damage Types](../rules/damage-types.md).
- **Levels 2, 4, 6:** Your maximum HP increases by an amount equal to your character level. (Each even level of this
  track adds this bonus once.)
