# Physical Fitness Programs

## Weight Training 1-4

Increase Strength by 1 per level.

## Agility Training 1-4

Increase Dexterity by 1 per level.

## Fortitude Training 1-4

Increase Constitution by 1 per level.

## Physical Saves Training 1-4

*Each level once per save.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution saves.
- **Level 2:** +1 to all those saves.
- **Level 3:** +1 to all those saves.
- **Level 4:** Advantage on all those saves.

## Physical Skills Training 1-5

*Each level once per skill.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Athletics, Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, or Stealth.
- **Level 2:** +1 to checks with that skill.
- **Level 3:** Ignore a critical failure in that skill (long rest).
- **Level 4:** +1 to checks.
- **Level 5:** Ignore a critical failure (short rest).

## Cardio Training 1-4

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Athletics, or Expertise if already proficient.
- **Level 2:** +5 ft movement.
- **Level 3:** Dash as a bonus action.
- **Level 4:** +5 ft movement.

## Obstacle Course Trained 1-4

- **Level 1:** Climbing doesn't cost extra movement.
- **Level 2:** Standing from prone costs only 10 ft.
- **Level 3:** Running long/high jump after only 5 ft (instead of 10 ft).
- **Level 4:** +1d4 to all Acrobatics and Athletics checks.

## Resistance Training 1-6

- **Levels 1, 3, 5:** Resistance to one damage type.
- **Levels 2, 4, 6:** +1 HP per level.
