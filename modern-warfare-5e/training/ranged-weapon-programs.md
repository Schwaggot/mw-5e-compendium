# Ranged Weapon Programs

## Automatic Weapons Training 1-5

- **Level 1:** During Riddle and Spray firing modes, only suffer -4 per Target/Volley.
- **Level 2:** Nominate Dex modifier + 1 when determining max targets/volleys during Riddle and Spray.
- **Level 3:** Only -3 per Target/Volley.
- **Level 4:** Fire max rounds during each burst; add 1 damage die to damage rolls.
- **Level 5:** Only -2 per Target/Volley.

## Burst Fire Training 1-5

- **Level 1:** -4 per Burst/Controlled Burst.
- **Level 2:** -3 per Burst/Controlled Burst.
- **Level 3:** -2 per Burst/Controlled Burst.
- **Level 4:** -1 per Burst/Controlled Burst.
- **Level 5:** No negatives for Burst or Controlled Burst.

## Marksmanship Technique Training 1-4

- **Level 1:** At long range, count yourself as firing at 1 less range multiplier (min 1).
- **Level 2:** Aim as a bonus action.
- **Level 3:** Before a ranged attack with a proficient weapon, take -5 to attack; on hit, +10 to damage.
- **Level 4:** Count enemies as having one grade less of cover.

## Target Training 1-4

*Prereq: Marksman Level 2.*

- **Level 1:** Targeting a body part, reduce penalty by 1.
- **Level 2:** Add Proficiency bonus to any DC of your targeted shots.
- **Level 3:** Reduce body-part targeting penalty by an additional 1.
- **Level 4:** Targets have disadvantage on saves against your targeted shots.

## Close Quarters Marksmanship 1-4

- **Level 1:** +1 to attack rolls within one range increment.
- **Level 2:** +1 to damage rolls within one range increment.
- **Level 3:** +2 to attack rolls within one range increment.
- **Level 4:** +2 to damage rolls within one range increment.

## Long Range Marksmanship 1-4

- **Level 1:** +1 to attack rolls at greater than one range increment.
- **Level 2:** +1 to damage rolls at greater than one range increment.
- **Level 3:** +2 to attack rolls at greater than one range increment.
- **Level 4:** +2 to damage rolls at greater than one range increment.

## Guns Akimbo Training 1-4

*Prereq: Dexterity 16+.*

- **Level 1:** Offhand bonus-action attack with pistols that don't have Light.
- **Level 2:** +1 to attack and damage from either hand when wielding 2 ranged weapons.
- **Level 3:** Two basic attacks with your offhand as a bonus action.
- **Level 4:** Dual wield two-handed ranged weapons but fire at disadvantage.

## Ranged Weapon Training 1-5

*Each level may be taken once per Weapon Proficiency.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in one ranged or melee weapon group.
- **Level 2:** +1 to all attack rolls in that group.
- **Level 3:** +1 to all damage rolls in that group.
- **Level 4:** +1 to all attack rolls in that group.
- **Level 5:** +1 to all damage rolls in that group.
