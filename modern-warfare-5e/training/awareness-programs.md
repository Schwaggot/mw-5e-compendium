# Awareness Programs

Awareness programs sharpen an operator's senses: spotting threats first, avoiding ambushes, and keeping the crew's head
on a swivel during movement. Passive Perception = 10 + WIS mod + any relevant proficiencies; the bonuses below stack
with it.

## Awareness Training 1-4

Baseline sensory conditioning. Makes you harder to sneak up on and better at finding things that are deliberately
hidden.

- **Level 1:** Dim light no longer imposes disadvantage on WIS (Perception) checks relying on sight.
- **Level 2:** +5 to passive Perception.
- **Level 3:** Advantage on WIS (Perception) and INT (Investigation) checks to find secret doors and hidden caches.
- **Level 4:** An additional +5 to passive Perception (total +10 from this track).

## Advanced Awareness Training 1-4

*Prereq: Awareness Training Level 2.*

Reaction speed and tactical awareness. Lets you read a situation faster than the people trying to jump you.

- **Level 1:** Advantage on initiative rolls.
- **Level 2:** Traveling at a fast pace (normally -5 to passive Perception) no longer imposes that penalty.
- **Level 3:** You cannot be surprised while conscious.
- **Level 4:** If you can hear, you know the location (but not identity) of any hidden or invisible creature within 20
  ft.

## Counter Ambush Training 1-4

Hardening against the ambush scenario itself: harder to catch flat-footed, and harder to hurt when caught.

- **Level 1:** Advantage on WIS (Perception) and INT (Investigation) checks to detect traps or impending surprise
  attacks.
- **Level 2:** Traveling at a fast pace no longer imposes the -5 penalty on passive Perception.
- **Level 3:** Resistance to all damage dealt during a surprise round against you.
- **Level 4:** +5 AC against attacks from creatures you cannot see.
