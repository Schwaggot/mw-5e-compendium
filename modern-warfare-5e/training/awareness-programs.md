# Awareness Programs

## Awareness Training 1-4

- **Level 1:** Dim light doesn't impose disadvantage on Wis (Perception) checks relying on sight.
- **Level 2:** +5 passive perception.
- **Level 3:** Advantage on Wis (Perception) and Int (Investigation) vs secret doors and hidden caches.
- **Level 4:** +5 passive perception.

## Advanced Awareness Training 1-4

*Prereq: Awareness Training Level 2.*

- **Level 1:** Advantage on initiative rolls.
- **Level 2:** Fast travel doesn't impose the -5 penalty on passive Perception.
- **Level 3:** Can't be surprised while conscious.
- **Level 4:** If you can hear, you know the location of any hidden/invisible creature within 20 ft.

## Counter Ambush Training 1-4

- **Level 1:** Advantage on Wis (Perception)/Int (Investigation) vs traps or surprise attacks.
- **Level 2:** Fast travel doesn't impose the -5 penalty on passive Perception.
- **Level 3:** Resistance to damage dealt during a Surprise.
- **Level 4:** +5 AC vs attacks from unseen enemies.
