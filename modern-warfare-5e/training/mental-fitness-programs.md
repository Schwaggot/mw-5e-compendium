# Mental Fitness Programs

## Education Training 1-4

Increase Intelligence by 1 per level.

## Resolve Training 1-4

Increase Wisdom by 1 per level.

## Social Training 1-4

Increase Charisma by 1 per level.

## Mental Saves Training 1-4

*Each level once per save.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saves.
- **Level 2:** +1 to those saves.
- **Level 3:** +1 to those saves.
- **Level 4:** Advantage on those saves.

## Mental Skills Training 1-5

*Each level once per skill.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in History, Investigation, Nature, Religion, Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Perception, Survival, Deception, Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion.
- **Level 2:** +1 to checks.
- **Level 3:** Ignore a critical failure (long rest).
- **Level 4:** +1 to checks.
- **Level 5:** Ignore a critical failure (short rest).

## Language Training 1-5

*Each level once per language.*

| Task  | Check        |
|-------|--------------|
| Speak | Charisma     |
| Write | Dexterity    |
| Read  | Intelligence |

- **Level 1:** Basic: pass a DC 10 check.
- **Level 2:** Moderate: DC 15.
- **Level 3:** Expert: DC 10 (the original text inverts the progression).
- **Level 4:** Master: no test needed.
- **Level 5:** Lip-read a known language.
