# Armor Proficiency Programs

## Light Armor Training 1-4

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Lite armor.
- **Level 2:** +1 AC while wearing Lite.
- **Level 3:** Expertise in Dex saves.
- **Level 4:** +1 AC while wearing Lite.

## Medium Armor Training 1-4

*Note: the source text says Proficiency in Heavy armor at Level 1, which appears to be a typo.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Medium armor (as written in source: Heavy).
- **Level 2:** +HP equal to your Proficiency.
- **Level 3:** Expertise in Con saves.
- **Level 4:** +HP equal to your Proficiency × 2.

## Heavy Armor Training 1-4

*Note: source text says Proficiency in Medium armor, another apparent typo.*

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Heavy armor (source says Medium).
- **Level 2:** +1 DR for each piece of coverage.
- **Level 3:** Expertise in Strength saves.
- **Level 4:** +1 additional DR per piece of coverage.
