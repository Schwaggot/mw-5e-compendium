# Armor Proficiency Programs

Armor programs teach an operator how to wear and fight in increasingly heavy protective gear. Each track covers one
weight class; the weight classes are defined by the soft-vest and plate combinations in [Armor](../armor.md). You must
be proficient in a given class of armor to wear it without penalty (disadvantage on STR/DEX checks and saves, attack
rolls, and half movement).

"Expertise" on a save, as used below, means you add double your proficiency bonus instead of the normal single bonus.

## Light Armor Training 1-4

Training for Concealable Vests and Light Plate Carriers: how to move quickly in soft armor without it slowing you down.

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Light armor.
- **Level 2:** +1 AC while wearing Light armor.
- **Level 3:** Expertise on Dexterity saves while wearing Light armor.
- **Level 4:** An additional +1 AC while wearing Light armor (total +2 from this track).

## Medium Armor Training 1-4

Training for Medium Flak / IBA-class rigs: learning to carry weight without losing wind.

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Medium armor.
- **Level 2:** Gain bonus HP equal to your proficiency bonus while wearing Medium armor.
- **Level 3:** Expertise on Constitution saves while wearing Medium armor.
- **Level 4:** Bonus HP equal to twice your proficiency bonus while wearing Medium armor (replaces Level 2).

## Heavy Armor Training 1-4

Training for fully-loaded flak and EOD-grade kit. Teaches you to turn the armor itself into a weapon - soaking hits with
the plates rather than dodging them.

- **Level 1:** Proficiency in Heavy armor.
- **Level 2:** +1 DR for each piece of coverage on your armor (see Coverage column in [Armor](../armor.md)).
- **Level 3:** Expertise on Strength saves while wearing Heavy armor.
- **Level 4:** An additional +1 DR per piece of coverage (total +2 per piece from this track).
