## Chest Rigs

Chest rigs are worn over clothing and hold magazines and admin pouches *without* ballistic plates.
A [plate carrier](../armor.md#plate-carriers) provides the same MOLLE attachment points plus armor - some Operators wear
a chest rig over a plate carrier, while others pick a plate carrier with integrated rig features (Crye AVS, LBT-6094,
Eagle CIRAS) and skip the separate rig entirely.

| Item                | Weight | Tags | Notes                                          |
|---------------------|--------|------|------------------------------------------------|
| Chest rig (generic) | 0.8 kg | -    | Worn over torso; holds mags and admin pouches. |
| Crye CPC            | 0.7 kg | -    | Minimalist combat pack-compatible chest rig.   |
| LBT-1961G           | 0.9 kg | -    | Classic SOF chest rig.                         |
| Mayflower APC       | 0.8 kg | -    | Low-profile assault panel.                     |

## Belts and Pouches

The battle belt overlaps with the riggers and pistol belts under [Clothing - Belts](clothing.md#belts). The
difference is padding and MOLLE-compatibility: a battle belt is built to mount pouches and a holster, while a riggers or
pistol belt serves the same role with clip-on attachments. Pick one belt system, not both. Most pouches listed here are
modular and also mount on the chest rigs above or on a [plate carrier](../armor.md#plate-carriers).
