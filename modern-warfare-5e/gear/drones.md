# Drones

Unmanned aerial systems available to the unit. See [Gear Tags](../gear.md#gear-tags) for tag definitions.

## B-1 Recon Drone

The B-1 Recon Drone (B1-RD or "bird") is a small man-portable 4-rotor drone weighing ~3.6 kg including its command
console. Deployed as an action. When operating, you command the drone via a 2-handed controller/screen. This uses your
full attention - you may not move, act, or react while piloting.

- Only passive skills apply during operation.
- Drone vision grants +5 Perception through the drone.
- DC 15 DEX (B1-RD tools) check to move the drone 60 ft; DC 20 for 120 ft dash. On fail, move half that turn (plus
  disadvantage on perception).
- Hover as a free action; land on a surface within 60 ft as a bonus action.
- Stats: AC 15, 10 HP, DEX saves use operator DEX + Prof (if proficient).
- Battery: 1 hour flight. Recharge with a long rest + power source.
- If destroyed and recovered: short rest + Drone Repair Kit + DC 15 Technology (INT) check (+1 per 20 ft fallen). Fail =
  one more attempt during a long rest at DC 20 + fall modifier. Second fail = unrepairable.

See [Tech Specialist](../designations/tech-specialist.md) for drone upgrades, enhanced variants, and operator bonuses.
