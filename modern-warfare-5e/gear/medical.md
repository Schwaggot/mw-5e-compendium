# Medical Supplies

Trauma and field-medical equipment. Mechanical rules for trauma items live with
the [Combat Medic](../designations/combat-medic.md) designation; most items on this page are narrative/roleplay props -
a medic uses them to *examine*, *diagnose*, *treat*, and *document* as much as to restore HP.
See [Gear Tags](../gear.md#gear-tags) for tag definitions.
