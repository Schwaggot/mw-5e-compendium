# Armor and Protective Gear

For protective gloves and boots, see [Clothing - Gloves](clothing.md#gloves)
and [Clothing - Footwear](clothing.md#footwear).

## Stopping Power (NIJ Levels)

Armor in this compendium is rated by the NIJ standard. The **Stops** column on every table below refers to the soft
panel's rating; rifle plate stopping power is listed separately under [Plates](#plates).

| Level | Stops                                                               |
|-------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| IIA   | 9mm, .40 S&W (subsonic pistol).                                     |
| II    | 9mm +P, .357 Magnum (standard pistol).                              |
| IIIA  | .357 SIG, .44 Magnum, most handguns and shotgun slugs (heavy soft). |
| III   | 7.62×51 FMJ and most standard rifle rounds.                         |
| III+  | + 5.56 M855 and 7.62×39 MSC "green tip" steel-core.                 |
| IV    | .30-06 AP and most armor-piercing rifle rounds.                     |

AC bonus and DR from armor/plates apply only to the side/slot they cover. The GM adjudicates which piece catches a given
hit.
